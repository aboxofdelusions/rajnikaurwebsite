About Rajni Kaur

Rajni Kaur is a poet and writer whose work explores the quiet, often unspoken corners of the human mind. Drawing on themes of anxiety, self-discovery, and resilience, her debut collection invites readers to confront their own hidden rooms with honesty and tenderness.
